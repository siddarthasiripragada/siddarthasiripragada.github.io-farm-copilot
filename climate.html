🔄 Climate Adaptation Practices
No-Till / Reduced Tillage
Improves soil water retention by 15–25%. Reduces fuel costs and soil erosion. Builds organic matter over time. Carbon credit eligible.
Cover Cropping
Winter rye, oats, or legume mixes protect soil between cash crops. Fixes nitrogen, suppresses weeds, improves soil biology.
Wetland Restoration
Restoring marginal land to wetlands improves flood resilience and attracts RALP funding ($14.6M Ontario pool). Carbon sequestration benefit.
