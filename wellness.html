💡 Farmer Wellness Tips
Financial Stress
Separate your farm's financial situation from your self-worth. Bad years happen — they don't define you as a farmer or a person. Talk to your bank about AgriStability before skipping a payment.
Isolation
Schedule one non-farm social event per week, even a phone call. Rural isolation is a measurable health risk. The Young Agrarians peer network runs virtual circles for farmers across Canada.
Physical Health
Farm injuries peak during planting and harvest. Take 15-minute breaks every 2 hours of equipment operation. Fatigue and stress are leading causes of farm accidents.
Asking for Help
Seeking support is a sign of strength, not weakness. The 1-866-FARMS01 line is staffed by counsellors who grew up on farms. They understand production losses, succession stress, and rural identity.
