AgriInsurance — Canada's Production Insurance Program

Federal-provincial cost-shared crop insurance covers production losses from natural hazards: drought, excess moisture, frost, pests, and disease. Premiums are shared: ~40% farmer, ~40% provincial, ~20% federal. Apply through your provincial crop insurance corporation before the planting deadline.

💡 Key Reminders
⏱ You must enrol before planting to be covered for that crop year
📋 Report seeded acreage within required window after seeding
📞 Report losses within 72 hours of occurrence
📁 Keep yield records — they determine your APH and future premiums
💰 AgriStability and crop insurance can both be held simultaneously
