⚖️ Your Rights as a Farm Worker in Canada
Your Rights — Federal Law
✅ Right to be paid at least provincial minimum wage
✅ Right to a written employment contract (TFW)
✅ Right to safe working conditions (OHS)
✅ Right to medical care if injured at work (WCB)
✅ Right to keep your passport — employer cannot hold it
✅ Right to contact ESDC without employer permission
✅ No fees can be charged by recruiters or employers
If Your Rights Are Violated
📞 Call the Migrant Worker Support Network: 1-888-440-4443
📞 ESDC TFW Tipline: 1-800-367-5693
📞 Provincial Labour Standards offices
⚠️ You cannot be deported for reporting a violation
🏠 Housing Standards
🏠 Employer-provided housing must meet provincial building codes
🔥 Functioning heat — min 18°C indoor temperature
💧 Potable running water and functioning bathroom facilities
🛏 Minimum space per person as defined by provincial standards
🚪 You must be able to leave the property during non-work hours
💰 Housing deduction from wages cannot exceed provincial maximum
🔒 Your employer cannot lock you in your housing
If housing conditions are unsafe, contact ESDC or your provincial ag ministry. You have the right to leave without losing your work permit.
