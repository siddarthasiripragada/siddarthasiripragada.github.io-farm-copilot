📋 Equipment Sharing Agreement — Key Elements
Essentials to Include
✅ Equipment description, make, model, year, serial number
✅ Rental rate and payment schedule
✅ Pickup/return responsibilities and delivery costs
✅ Insurance — who covers during rental?
✅ Damage liability threshold before deposit is claimed
✅ Fuel: full-to-full policy or separate billing
✅ Hours/acre limits per rental period
✅ Cancellation notice period
Common Pitfalls to Avoid
⚠️ Unclear who covers breakdowns during use
⚠️ No written confirmation of pre-rental condition
⚠️ Missing harvest schedule conflicts clause
⚠️ No emergency access override for owner's own harvest
⚠️ Ambiguous end-of-season cleaning standards
⚠️ No dispute resolution process specified
⚠️ Verbal-only agreement — always use written contracts
